# Portfolio-Website-App
This project is a personal website designed to showcase your skills, projects, and achievements in a professional and visually appealing way. The app serves as a digital resume and portfolio, allowing visitors to learn about your background, explore your work, and easily connect with you.

Basic Idea
Home Page: A welcoming introduction with your name, tagline, and highlights.

About Section: A brief overview of your background, interests, and career goals.

Projects Showcase: Organized display of your key projects with descriptions, screenshots, or links.

Contact Page: Simple form or links for visitors to reach out via email or social platforms.

Responsive Design: Works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices.

Purpose
The Portfolio Website App helps you build a strong online presence, making it easier for recruiters, collaborators, or clients to understand your capabilities and connect with you. It’s essentially your professional identity online, presented in a clean and accessible format.
